HOW TO PLAY
Guess how to spell PEGOR in six tries.

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was.

Examples

P A N T S
The letter P is in the word and in the correct spot.

T A S T E
The letter A is in the word but in the wrong spot.

D A N C E
The letter C is not in the word in any spot.

Same PEGORDLE every single day! ;)
